About Me

Hello! I am Ross Ferguson. Currently, I am a Physics teacher living and working on the east coast of Scotland. At present, I am developing my skills as a full-stack web developer by completing a Full-stack Bootcamp offered by HyperionDev and the University of Edinburgh.
As a teacher and self-certified geek, I am extremely passionate about the use of technology in the classroom and how we can develop and foster our young people to become the developers and engineers of the future.
In terms of design, I am inspired by simple colour schemes with bold splashes of colour. I also enjoy retro and space-inspired themes taking inspiration from sci-fi books and movies.
My goal in the future is to combine my passion for education and teaching with my newfound passion for web development. I would love to work for a company that shared these passions and endeavoured to provide a first-class user experience.

Projects

As a web developer my most recent and personally rewarding project has been to create an online store for a fictional business.
The store had to meet several criteria such as;

  • Have a functional shopping cart
  • Allow customers to apply a working coupon and update the total to reflect the change
  • Have a page which shows the cart
  • Allow the user to select a delivery method and update the total cost to reflect the cost of the method chosen
  • Have an individual page for each product
I also decided to challenge myself by making it so that I would have one page for the products and this would update depending on which item from the catalogue the user had selected.
I managed to achieve all of the above receiving full marks and positive comments from the code the reviewer who had reviewed my work.
I am happy to share the full project on GitHub if requested.
